Maid

Local AI Chat for Android

A free, open-source Android app that lets you chat with AI models entirely on your device — no subscription, no cloud, no data leaving your phone.

Features

Local & Remote Models

Run GGUF models on-device via llama.cpp, or connect to Ollama, OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, and DeepSeek with your own API key.

One-Tap Downloads

Browse and download curated Hugging Face models directly within the app — no manual file management needed.

Custom Model Support

Load any GGUF file from local storage. Full control over which model you run.

Configurable Parameters

Adjust temperature, top-p, top-k, context length, and other generation settings per session.

Chat Management

Create, rename, delete, export, and import conversations as JSON files.

Voice Integration

Pairs with Maise for on-device text-to-speech. Hear your AI respond without sending audio to the cloud.

Used Around the World

When the internet goes down — or when going online isn't safe — Maid keeps working. Because everything runs on the device, users in the most difficult circumstances in the world have come to rely on it.

Active Conflict Zones

People in Ukraine and Gaza have used Maid to access AI assistance when internet infrastructure is destroyed, unreliable, or actively monitored. No connection means no exposure — the model runs entirely on the phone.

First Responders

Emergency services and first responders operating in remote or disaster-affected areas use Maid as an AI reference tool when mobile data is unavailable or too slow to be useful.

Off-Grid & Remote

From rural communities to offshore vessels, Maid works anywhere an Android phone works — no Wi-Fi, no cell signal, no problem.
